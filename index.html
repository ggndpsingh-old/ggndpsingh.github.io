THE LOWDOWN

My name is Gagan, but around these parts I go by Gags. I make iOS apps, but I do work in other mediums as well. I currently reside in New Delhi, India with a couple of startup founders.

My academic education is in Information Technology and Business Management (I am always more than happy to have an intelligent conversation about anything, especially Advanced Swift these days, so feel free to hit me up) and I've spent a few years in retail and as a web developer before becoming an iOS developer. All of these experiences play a heavy part in what I do, how I do it and who I am in general.

Beyond the endless nights of coding, I play snooker and I'll never pass up an early morning photography session from a mountain top. I enjoy a few TV shows but GOT isn't one of them. Also, I like beer.
